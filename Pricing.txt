Pricing 
   * Payment Methods:
     - Cash (on delivery or pickup)
     - Card reader (on delivery or pickup; €0.50 service charge applies)
     - Do not accept card details over the phone.
   * Delivery Charge: Determined by the **Delivery Area** list.
   * Product Price: Base Price + Options Cost
     - Options Cost:
       - "+€Number" = additional cost (e.g., +€2).
       - "-€Number" = reduction cost (e.g., -€2).
   * Total Order Price:
     - By Cash = Sum of (Quantity × Product Price) + Delivery Charge
     - By Card Reader = Sum of (Quantity × Product Price) + Delivery Charge + €0.50
   * Minimum Order Price: €10 (including Delivery Charge)
